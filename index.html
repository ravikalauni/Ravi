MY LATEST PROJECT

As my curiosity about AI chatbots grew, I started exploring how they work and wondered if I could build something similar. But soon, I realized it wasn't as easy as I thought — it takes serious technical skills, a solid team, a decent budget, and a lot more. Still, I didn't give up. I dived into understanding the basic algorithms and got a sense of how the core systems work.

That's when I built a simple chatbot called "Gudiya." It's not some advanced AI, just a basic JavaScript project — but it's personal. Gudiya evaluates the people I know using documents I've prepared to train it. When a user enters their name, Gudiya tells them how I know them, when we met, and what our relationship is. It even checks if the person is connected with me on social media or not.

It's not a big project in the grand scheme of things, but when you build something with your own hands and it actually works — that hits different. Gudiya now works as my own little personal assistant.
