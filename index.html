Gudiya - Personal AI Assistant

As my curiosity about AI chatbots grew, I started exploring how they work and wondered if I could build something similar. While I realized it takes serious technical skills, a solid team, and resources to build advanced AI, I didn't give up.

Gudiya is my personal JavaScript chatbot that evaluates people I know based on my training data. It provides information about how I know someone, when we met, and our relationship status.
