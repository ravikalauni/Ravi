PRIVACY POLICY

Ravi's Privacy

Ravi values his privacy, and as such, he has not shared any personal or sensitive information on this website. The content shared here consists only of stories and experiences that Ravi believes should be shared with others. Any personal information is securely protected, and to access such details, a password is required, which can only be obtained by directly contacting Ravi.

Information Collection

We respect your privacy too! Your information is never shared. We do not use third-party APIs or cookies to store your data. If you choose to share any personal information, such as your name, email, or phone number, it will be stored in our database only with your consent. We will never use your information without your permission. Additionally, this website does not run ads, so we do not use your information to show you targeted advertisements.

We might know you!

If you know Ravi or have interacted with him in the past, our system may recognize you. Our AI integration collects data from the stories shared on the website, and in some cases, you may be asked to enter your name to verify that you are part of Ravi’s circle. Don’t worry—this name is only used for verification and is not stored in the database. If you are a friend, relative, family member, neighbor, teacher, or someone close to Ravi, your name might appear in the system. However, it will not be made public without your explicit consent.

No signup or login

There is no mandatory sign-up or login required to access the website. You can browse the site freely without creating an account. However, Ravi does log in with a password to access the admin section of the website, where he can post updates and new content.

Your privacy is important, and Ravi takes all necessary measures to ensure it is protected.
