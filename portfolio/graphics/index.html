About Me

I am a graphic designer with over 3 years of experience creating visual identities, marketing materials, and digital assets for brands across various industries. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to deliver designs that not only look beautiful but also solve real business problems.



Experience

I have hands-on experience in graphic design, working on a wide range of projects including posters, social media graphics, logos, and branding materials. My focus has been on creating clear, visually consistent designs that meet specific goals and requirements. I am proficient in tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva, and I have developed a solid understanding of layout design, typography, and color theory. I am capable of managing design tasks from concept to final output while meeting deadlines and maintaining quality standards.

Alongside my academic studies, I have developed strong skills in graphic design through online courses and practical experience.
